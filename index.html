02 · The Access Gap

The spiritual care access gap

Trusted spiritual care is hard to access.

People have three places to turn, and each falls short of the same thing: steady, trusted, one-on-one spiritual care.

The local church

Essential, but carrying more than it can hold. Many lack the capacity, systems, or trained guides for steady one-on-one care through the week.

72% of senior pastors have seriously considered resigning from burnout (Barna)

Therapy

Vital and valued, but not designed for discipleship or spiritual formation.

60% of psychologists have zero openings, 3+ month waits (APA) · 169M live in shortage areas (HRSA)

Faith content

More than ever, yet it cannot know you, listen, pray with you, or help you discern your next step.
